  • Once someone kills you, your target becomes their next one.

Weapon

  • Plastic spoons.

  • You are SAFE while:

  • Research, PLTL, BTL, PCS tutoring is considered class.

  • You are at a prayer or religious event.
  • You are working and on or off-campus job.
